tales from the loop

[design]

role : project manager and designer

Tales from the loop is a boardgame inspired by the graphic universe of Simon Stålenhag. With a team of 6 students, from HETIC, we designed a website for the launch of the game. The main purposes were to introduce the game and create the desire to purchase it.

From the idea to its creation and release, it took us 4 days.

Collaborators : Bich Tram Cynthia Pham, Charlotte Siees, Thomas Hénault, Duke Habbib and Simon Lesueur.
