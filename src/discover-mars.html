discover mars

[website]

role : front-end developer

Discover Mars is a website intented to introduce to the general public the planet Mars and its specificities. Thanks to the data recovered by the robot Curiosity, the purpose of this website is to arouse the desire for learning and stir the visitor's curiosity. Day after day, you can see data collected by the robot and take a closer look at the red planet. It was created by 5 students from HETIC.

From the idea to its creation and release, it took us 4 days.

Collaborators : Kelly Phan, Simon Lucas, Léo Guichet and Lucile Van Severen.
